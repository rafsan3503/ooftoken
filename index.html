Introducing OOF

OOF" is a term popularized within the Roblox gaming community, originating from the sound effect used in the game when a character dies. In Roblox, when a player's character takes damage or is defeated, the game plays the sound "oof," which is a short, humorous exclamation.

"OOF"meme is brought into memecoin space to take advantage of its memability for people.
